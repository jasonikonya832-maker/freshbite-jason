Receive your order at the estimated delivery time. Fresh & delicious!

🛍️ Step 1: Shopping

  • Go to Shop: Click the "Shop" link in the navigation menu or click "Shop Now" on the homepage.
  • Browse Products: You'll see all our items with names, prices, and beautiful food emojis. We have 4 types of cookies and 5 types of fresh fruits.
  • Filter by Category: Use the buttons at the top:
    • "All" - See everything
    • "🍪 Cookies" - See our homemade cookies
    • "🍎 Fruits" - See fresh fruits
  • View Details: Click on any product to see more information about it.
  • Add to Cart: When you find something you like, click the "Add to Cart" button. You'll see a green notification confirming the item was added.
💡 Pro Tip: You can add different items from different visits - your cart is saved! The cart count in the top right shows how many items you have.

🛒 Step 2: Review Your Cart

  • Go to Cart: Click the "Cart" link in the top navigation menu.
  • See Your Items: All your selected items are listed with:
    • Product picture (emoji)
    • Product name
    • Unit price in KES
    • Quantity
    • Total for that item
  • Adjust Quantities: Use the + and - buttons to increase or decrease quantities. The total updates instantly!
  • Remove Items: Click the "Remove" button if you changed your mind. The item disappears instantly.
  • View Order Summary: At the bottom you'll see:
    • Subtotal: Total price of all items
    • Tax (16%): Government tax on purchase
    • Total: Subtotal + Tax
  • Proceed to Checkout: Click the blue "Proceed to Checkout" button when you're happy with your cart.
⚠️ Remember: Your cart is saved on your device. If you clear browser cookies or use a different device, your cart will be empty. If you leave and come back, your items will still be there!

📍 Step 3: Checkout & Delivery Details

  • Enter Shipping Information: Fill in your details:
    • First Name: Your first name
    • Last Name: Your last name
    • Email: Your email address (for order confirmation)
    • Phone: Your phone number (for delivery confirmation). Use format: +254712345678
    • Street Address: Your street address in detail
    • City: Nairobi (our service area)
    • Postal Code: Your postal code (e.g., 00100, 00200)
  • Select Delivery Zone: Click on one of our 7 delivery zones:
Delivery Zone Areas Covered Fee Estimated Time

  • Zone Selection Tips:
    • Click on a zone card to select it - it will turn orange with a checkmark
    • The delivery fee for that zone appears instantly
    • Your total updates automatically to include the delivery fee
    • Not sure? Check the "Areas Covered" column above
💡 Pro Tip: These are our standard delivery times. During peak hours or bad weather, it might take slightly longer. We always do our best to deliver on time!

💳 Step 4: Payment Method

  • Choose Your Payment Method: Select one of these options:
    • 📱 M-Pesa: Pay via M-Pesa (Most popular!). You'll receive payment instructions after confirming your order.
    • 📱 Airtel Money: Pay via Airtel Money. Same process as M-Pesa.
    • 💳 Credit/Debit Card: Use your Visa or Mastercard. Payment details provided after order.
    • 🏦 Bank Transfer: Transfer funds directly. Bank details provided after order.
    • 💵 Cash on Delivery: Pay when your order arrives! No payment needed online.
  • Recommended for You:
    • If you're in Central Nairobi → Cash on Delivery or M-Pesa
    • If you're far away → M-Pesa or Card (payment first, then delivery)
    • If you prefer online → Card or Bank Transfer
⚠️ Cash on Delivery Important: When using "Pay when order arrives" option, you must have the exact cash ready. Our driver will have limited change!

✅ Step 5: Place Your Order

  • Review Everything: Before clicking, check the right panel (Order Summary) shows:
    • All your items with quantities
    • Correct subtotal
    • Tax calculation (16%)
    • Your selected delivery zone
    • Delivery fee
    • Grand total
  • All Fields Required: You must fill in:
    • ✓ All shipping information (name, email, phone, address)
    • ✓ A delivery zone (required!)
    • ✓ A payment method (required!)
    If you miss anything, you'll get a friendly reminder.
  • Click "Place Order": The big blue button at the bottom. This submits your order.
  • Wait for Confirmation: The page will process for 1-2 seconds, then you'll see a confirmation page.
💡 Pro Tip: Don't refresh the page during checkout - wait for the confirmation to appear!

✨ Step 6: Order Confirmation

  • Confirmation Screen: You'll see a page that shows:
    • ✅ Big green checkmark (your order is confirmed!)
    • Your unique order number (save this!)
    • Delivery address and zone
    • Estimated delivery time
    • Payment method
    • Order total
  • Next Steps Based on Payment Method:
    • M-Pesa/Airtel/Card/Bank: You'll receive payment instructions via email. Follow them to pay.
    • Cash on Delivery: No payment needed now. Prepare cash for when driver arrives.
  • What Happens Next:
    • 📧 Email confirmation sent with order details
    • 📱 SMS to your phone with delivery zone and time frame
    • 🚚 Driver arrives within estimated time window
    • 🔔 You might receive a call 5 minutes before arrival
  • Click "Back to Home": When ready, return to shopping or go home. Your cart will be empty (ready for next order).
💡 Pro Tip: Save your order number! If there are any questions about delivery, mention this number.

⚡ Quick Tips for Success

  • ✓ Use your real phone number - we call 5 minutes before delivery
  • ✓ Enter your exact address - apartment/flat/complex names help!
  • ✓ Select the correct delivery zone - it affects your fee and time
  • ✓ For M-Pesa/Airtel, check your email for payment links quickly
  • ✓ Cash on Delivery → Have exact change ready
  • ✓ During peak hours (lunch/dinner), delivery might take longer
  • ✓ Bad weather? We still deliver but might take extra time
  • ✓ Your cart is saved - you can shop again anytime!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

💰 Are the prices in Kenyan Shillings (KES)? +
Yes! All prices are in KES (Kenyan Shillings). The total you see is what you pay (plus delivery fee, minus any promotions).
🚚 What if my area isn't listed in the zones? +
All zones cover different parts of Nairobi. If you're unsure which zone you belong to, contact us on WhatsApp: +254 700 123 456 or email: info@freshbite.co.ke. We'll help you find the right zone!
⏰ What are your delivery hours? +
We deliver Monday-Friday from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Saturday-Sunday from 11:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Sunday deliveries are limited. Orders placed after closing hours will be delivered the next business day.
💳 Which payment methods are safest? +
All our payment methods are safe! M-Pesa and Airtel Money are very popular and secure in Kenya. If you prefer not to pay online, choose "Cash on Delivery" - pay when your delivery arrives. No online payment needed!
📦 Can I modify my order after placing it? +
Contact us within 5 minutes of placing your order. If we haven't started preparing it, we can modify it. Call or WhatsApp: +254 700 123 456.
🚫 What if my order doesn't arrive on time? +
Our estimated times are usually accurate, but traffic and weather can affect delivery. We'll try to call you if we're running late. If you don't receive your order within 30 minutes of the estimated window, contact us immediately!
🤔 Can I place an order for someone else? +
Yes! Enter their delivery address, phone number, and email. We'll send them the confirmation. The driver will deliver to that address.
♻️ Are cookies and fruits fresh? +
Absolutely! Our cookies are homemade fresh daily. Fruits are sourced fresh from markets daily. We only deliver items at peak freshness - this is guaranteed!
🎁 Do you offer gifts or special orders? +
Yes! For special requests like gift packaging, custom quantities, or bulk orders, contact us at info@freshbite.co.ke or WhatsApp: +254 700 123 456.
