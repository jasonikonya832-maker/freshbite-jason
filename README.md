# FreshBite Cookies & Fruits - E-Commerce Website

A modern, mobile-friendly e-commerce website for **FreshBite**, a premium cookie and fresh fruit business in Nairobi, Kenya.

## 🌟 Features

### Pages
- **Homepage** - Attractive hero section with featured products and business highlights
- **Products Page** - Full catalog with category filtering (Cookies & Fruits)
- **Product Details** - Individual product pages with descriptions and quantity selector
- **Shopping Cart** - View and manage cart items with quantity adjustment
- **Checkout** - Multi-step checkout with customer & shipping information
- **Contact Page** - Contact form with WhatsApp integration and business information

### Functionality
- ✅ **Product Catalog** - 9 diverse products (4 cookies, 5 fruits)
- ✅ **Shopping Cart** - Add/remove items, adjust quantities
- ✅ **Cart Persistence** - Cart saved in browser (localStorage)
- ✅ **Price Calculation** - Automatic total, tax (16% VAT), and subtotal calculation
- ✅ **Category Filtering** - Filter by Cookies, Fruits, or view All
- ✅ **Responsive Design** - Works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- ✅ **WhatsApp Integration** - Direct WhatsApp contact buttons
- ✅ **Order Confirmation** - Order confirmation page after checkout
- ✅ **Mobile Menu** - Hamburger menu for mobile navigation

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Open the Website
Simply open `index.html` in your web browser:
- Double-click `index.html` to open it, OR
- Right-click → Open with → Your preferred browser

### 2. Navigation
- Use the navigation menu to browse pages
- Click on products to view details
- Add items to cart from product cards or detail pages
- Click the cart icon to view your shopping cart

### 3. Shopping Process
1. Browse products on the Products page
2. Click on a product to view details
3. Select quantity and click "Add to Cart"
4. View cart by clicking the shopping cart icon
5. Proceed to checkout
6. Fill in shipping information
7. Select payment method
8. Place your order

### 4. Contact
- Use the Contact form to send inquiries
- Contact form integrates with WhatsApp
- Click WhatsApp button for instant messaging
